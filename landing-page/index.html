WebApp Introduction

Created as a Web Application for IT Elective 3, this student attendance monitoring system provides teachers and professors an easy and reliable medium for tallying and monitoring students' attendance. Furthermore, students can also interact with their teachers and vice versa thru an online chatbox system where they can appeal to their attendances.

WebApp Purpose and Functionalities

Student Attendance Monitoring System is created to lessen the burden of these teachers in tallying and monitoring the lates, absences and present days of their students..

  • Accessible to teachers and students
  • Less Hassle and more Energy saved
  • Wi-Fi compatible
  • User-Friendly Settings
  • Monitor and Edit at your own home
  • Coded by AdDU Products
